CURRICULUM & INSTRUCTION DIVISION (CID)

The Curriculum and Instructional Division is committed to providing a wide range of programs that
uphold the fundamental principles of PISAY, which are Academic Excellence, Patriotism, Integrity, Service,
and Youth Leadership, and help students reach their full potential. This division is dedicated to creating
educational programs that uphold the core values of the school's mission in addition to encouraging academic performance.
The section seeks to promote students' holistic development through unique and thorough methods.

>

STUDENT AND SERVICES DIVISION (SSD)

In charge of several school branches, the Student Services Division prioritizes the welfare
of its students while concentrating on providing specialized services and advantages. Its crucial
function is to establish a nurturing atmosphere that improves the general welfare of the student body.
The division is committed to offering a thorough framework for interaction and support of students.

>

FINANCE & ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICES DIVISION (FAD)

Overseeing financial and administrative operations, the Division of Finance and
Administration is essential to the school system. This covers duties including accounting,
budgeting, and overseeing contracts and purchases of products and services. The division supports
the overall operation of the educational system by ensuring transparent and efficient procedures.
