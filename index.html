COULD YOU GIVE A FEW EXAMPLES OF HOW YOUR SCHOOL ENCOURAGES STUDENTS'
HOLISTIC GROWTH, ENCOMPASSING EXTRACURRICULAR ACTIVITIES
AND SOCIAL SKILLS TO GUARANTEE THAT THEY ARE WELL-ROUNDED INDIVIDUALS?

The Philippine Science High School - Caraga Region Campus (PSHS-CRC) guarantees equal access to extracurricular activities, including math and scientific competitions. Additionally, moral education is an example of this is the Values Education Course, and community service projects help students develop their character and promote social responsibility. These efforts ensure that PSHS graduates become complete people with excellent interpersonal, intellectual, and social abilities.

WHAT INNOVATIVE TEACHING TECHNIQUES AND TECHNOLOGICAL RESOURCES DOES
YOUR SCHOOL USE TO ENGAGE STUDENTS' ATTENTION AND IMPROVE THEIR EDUCATION?

The curricular offerings of PSHS will be enhanced and firmly grounded in research and the science of learning. We must welcome more technological advancements and the digital revolution. We must reconsider how we offer teaching and evaluate it. To prepare students for the new ways that science, technology, and innovation will shape our lives in the digital age, PSHS must also instill in them an analytical mindset and foster their creative thinking. Additionally, the school uses technology, such as lab apparatus, to improve instruction and prepare students for careers in STEM.
