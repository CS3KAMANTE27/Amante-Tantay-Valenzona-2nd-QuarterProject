Quality Policy

The Philippine Science High School System
(PSHSS) is dedicated to providing relevant
secondary education with emphasis on science,
technology, engineering, and mathematics and
aimed at preparing students to become globally
competitive Filipino science and technology
leaders and professionals imbued with core
values of integrity, excellence, and service
to the nation. Moreover, the PSHSS is commited
to continual improvement and compliance
to existing statutory and regulatory
requirements.

Philosophy

The Philippine Science High School System (PSHSS) offers its students an education that enhances their knowledge in STEM while instilling core values, fostering academic and personal growth. Prioritizing the development of essential life skills and values, the school aims to cultivate a passion for learning, ensuring a successful future and career in science and technology while unlocking each scholar's full potential. Their goal is to nurture talents and harness them for national development. By integrating career-oriented teachings with the nurturing of core values such as Integrity, Excellence, and Service to Nation, the school ensures that students are not just academically equipped but also imbued with the qualities essential for success, paving the way towards a fulfilling and prosperous future.

About PSHS-CRC

The Philippine Science High School - Caraga Region Campus (PSHS-CRC) is the 13th campus in the Philippine Science High School System, situated in Brgy. Ampayon, Butuan City. It admits and offers scholarships to students excelling in science and mathematics. Renowned for its rigorous STEM program, the school engages in diverse research activities and competitions to nurture students' scientific inquiry and critical thinking. PSHS-CRC has a commendable history of success in local, regional, and international competitions, valuing academic excellence and innovation while shaping future leaders in science and technology.
