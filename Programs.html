INTRAMURALS

PSHS-CRC fosters an inclusive environment, allowing students to not
only enhance their skills but also to delve into the realms of enjoyment.
Intramurals gives the scholars an opportunity to unwind and enjoy the
thrill of competition, along with a supportive atmosphere. Giving them
a chance to make new connections and lasting memories.

STEM FEST

STEM Fests at PSHS-CRC showcase exploration, fostering an environment
for students to explore science and technology. With interactive exhibits
and competitions, these events cultivate curiosity and innovativeness,
reflecting the school's unwavering commitment to enhancing students'
STEM knowledge and skills for real-world application.

RED CROSS YOUTH (RCY)

PSHS-CRC provides scholars with the chance to participate in constructive
organizations such as the RCY. This opportunity allows students to engage
in humanitarian activities and promote service and unity. Through this,
students gain essential life skills and a sense of empowerment to
contribute to the well-being of both themselves and others.

CLUBS

PSHS-CRC offers a range of clubs to suit diverse interests, allowing
scholars to join those aligned with their aspirations and skills. As they
participate, students not only refine their skills but also make connections
with their peers and develop teamwork. These clubs support collaborative
learning and personal exploration in addition to developing talent.
